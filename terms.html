All owners of a Dretoni are covered under the following Terms of Service. By becoming the owner of a Dretoni, you agree to these terms.

    ➤ There is no cooldown

  • There is no amount of time you have to wait to transfer a character to someone else, unless the designer's TOS requests one.

    • ➤ Co-Ownership is allowed

  • The staff of Dretoni are not responsible for any issues that happen among co-owners. If you come to us with an issue in a ticket then we will close it with no responses.

    • ➺ It is not our issue, you must deal with it yourself.

    ➤ There is no resale/trade/gift policy

  • Again, unless the designer's TOS, a Dretoni can be resold or traded for whatever the owner wishes, even if they were gifted. Just please, do not scalp as that will earn a strike.

    • ➺ Unused MYOs may not be sold for greater than their market value.

    ➤ Multiple forms cannot be separated

  • If a Dretoni has a human form and a dretoni form for example, they cannot be rehomed separately unless the designer's TOS permits otherwise.

    • ➺ You may make alternative forms freely.

✧･ﾟ： ＊✧Ｒｉｇｈｔｓ✧＊：･ﾟ✧

    ➤ MYOs are pre set in rarity

  • MYOs can exist for any rarity. A MYOs rarity can be changed through items or paying the difference. MYOs can only go up in rarity, but you can use exclusively lower rarity traits if you wish. Myo slots are transferable but non-refundable.

    • ➺ MYOs do not expire. and Note: Official Artists cannot design myos.

    ➤ Dretoni MUST be approved to be used

  • In order to use a Dretoni, the designer must get them approved on discord as a MYO or redesign or in server as an official Artist

    • ➤ Rights can be revoked

  • An ill behaving Official Artist can have their rights revoked ONLY IF: They are blacklisted; they are a scammer; they are creating and distributing unapproved Dretoni designs. Revoking rights means the user will not be reimbursed for them either.

    • ➤ Rights may be traded, gifted, or sold

  • There is no standard to trading or gifting rights. In order to resell them, you MUST get them appraised by the Owner. It is on you, the rights holder, to keep record of your purchasing price and proof of payment for appraisal.

✧･ﾟ： ＊✧Ｄｅｓｉｇｎ✧＊：･ﾟ✧

    ➤ What is a redesign?

  • By Dretonin standards, a redesign is a change to the design to any degree that does not have to do with art style or re-refs.

    • ➤ How much can be redesigned?

  • Just about every part of a Dretoni can be redesigned, BUT traits may not be changed without items or rights. Additionally, if the overall design is changed over 50% or is overall unrecognizable to be the same character, it will require re-approval.

    • ➤ You do not need permission to redesign a Dretoni

  • Permission is not needed from moderators or from the designer, unless their TOS says otherwise. However, if a Dretoni reaches to "overhaul" amounts of changes, permission from the Designer is required and proof must be provided upon approval. If the designer has something in their TOS which permits overhauling, you can submit that as proof instead; permission to redesign a character in their TOS is not enough for overhaul purposes.

    • ➤ Does my redesign require approval?

  • Only if you are changing traits, or changing 50%+ of the design will a redesign need moderator approval.

    • ➤ Turning an OC into a Dretoni

  • You may turn a non-Dretoni oc, such as a pony or furry character, into a Dretoni freely. Unless the Designers TOS states that you may not create a semi-open species of their design, otherwise we do not require proof of permission but linking designers TOS is a must.
